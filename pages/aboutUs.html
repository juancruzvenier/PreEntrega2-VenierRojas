John Clayton Mayer, better known as John Mayer, is an American singer-songwriter, producer, and graphic designer who was born on October 16, 1977 in Bridgeport, Connecticut, United States, who ventured into the world of music in 1998 with genres such as pop rock, blue eyed soul and blues rock.
John has 8 albums and has won 7 music awards throughout his career, having been nominated 19 times.

Curious fact! The Grammy Award-winning song Daughters was composed by the artist in the shower.
